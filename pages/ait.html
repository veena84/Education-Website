ARMY INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (AIT) PUNE ADMISSION 2017

Army Institute of Technology, Pune has released notification for admission of 300 seats for Engineering Course commencing in July 2017. AIT Admission in based on the Rank obtained in the Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) Main 2017
Admission is offered only to the students who qualify the AIT Pune Admission 2017
Candidates can check more information about AIT Pune Admission like important dates, application form, eligibility criteria etc. on this page
For More Visit www.aitpune.com
